The origin

How PathFinder
came to be.

It started with a simple question that had no good answer: "What should I learn next?" Every search led to paid courses, Reddit rabbit holes, or overwhelming lists with zero structure.

"We didn't need another course. We needed a map — something that showed us where we were and where to go next."

— Ruhan Motiwale, Founder

So we built PathFinder. Not a platform. Not a course aggregator. A structured atlas for every skill — starting from zero, linking only the best free resources, and never putting anything behind a paywall.

🌱
The Problem

What we stand for

Our core
principles.

Every design decision, every roadmap, and every resource we curate is guided by these four values. They're not just words — they're the reason PathFinder exists.

🧭

Clarity First

No jargon. No assumed knowledge. Every roadmap starts at zero and builds logically — because confusion is the enemy of learning.

🌱

Beginner-Obsessed

We design everything with the most overwhelmed learner in mind. If it works for them, it works for everyone.

🆓

Radically Free

Knowledge access is a right, not a privilege. Every roadmap, resource link, and feature on PathFinder is and will remain permanently free.

🤝

Community-Driven

Our roadmaps are shaped by real learner feedback. If something doesn't work for the community, we fix it — together.
